Delete Your Account

Last updated: February 2026

You can delete your BirthNet account directly from the app. All your data will be permanently removed.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including profile, birthday records, and special days will be deleted immediately.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open BirthNet on your device and sign in.
2
Tap Settings (bottom right tab).
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account".
4
Confirm the deletion. Your account and all data will be permanently removed.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your phone number and profile information
  • Your birthday and special day records
  • Your profile photo
  • All app data associated with your account

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account from the app, contact us at: support@birthnetapp.com

We will process your deletion request within 30 days.
