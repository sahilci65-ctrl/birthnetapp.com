Child Safety Policy

Last updated: February 2025

BirthNet is committed to providing a safe experience for all users, including minors.

Our Commitment

BirthNet has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or harms minors. We strictly prohibit:

  • Any content that sexually exploits minors
  • Any grooming, trafficking, or exploitation of children
  • Any content that endangers the safety or well-being of minors

App Usage

BirthNet is a birthday and special day tracking app. The app does not contain any adult content, social messaging, or user-generated public content features beyond profile photos and birthday stories shared only with known contacts.

Reporting

If you encounter any content that violates this policy, please report it immediately to: safety@birthnetapp.com

We will investigate all reports and take appropriate action, including removing content and reporting to relevant authorities.

Contact

For child safety concerns: safety@birthnetapp.com
